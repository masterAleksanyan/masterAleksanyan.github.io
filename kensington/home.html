Featured community

malibu

Spanning 27 miles of Pacific Ocean coastline, this dazzling beachfront haven is recognized worldwide as one of Southern California's most famous and iconic residential locations. Malibu is populated with gorgeous oceanfront and coastal estates, prized by luxury home lovers and Hollywood celebrities alike.

Surfers flock to Malibu-the birthplace of California's legendary surfing scene-for its internationally regarded beaches including Surfrider Beach, Zuma Beach, and Topanga Beach. Rich with culture and history, this exclusive beach town also offers world-class ocean-side dining along the West Coast. Malibu isn't just a luxurious community full of exquisite properties and gorgeous beaches-it's a lifestyle.
